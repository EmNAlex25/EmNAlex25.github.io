For the animation of the montage I had remained with the theme of people being too involved with technology and missing events right in front of them. I made the people using their phones stationary, while the others are moving and having fun at their event. I used sounds such as ringing and buzzing and the person yelling "shut up!" in the midst of the crowd chatter to represent how frustrating it can be to attend an event where no one pays attention to their surroundings.
